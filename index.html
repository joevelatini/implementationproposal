Implementation Department Proposal

The Opportunity

As ActivTrak has moved upmarket into enterprise, our customer journey needs to evolve with us. Today, training is handled ad hoc by CSMs, which creates inconsistency and pulls CSMs away from strategic account work. A dedicated Implementation function would standardize the customer experience, improve time-to-value, and create a scalable model for growth.

Implementation Team Scope

Core Responsibilities

Onboarding Trainings Handle all structured training sessions during the launch period, ensuring customers reach competency before CSM handoff.
Tiered Training Packages Offer differentiated training based on customer segment and complexity. Enterprise clients receive more hands-on sessions; digital clients receive streamlined training with an option to upgrade for additional support.
Training Content Ownership Create, maintain, and update all training decks and materials. Ensure consistency across the customer base and keep content aligned with product updates.
Train the Trainer Programs Partner with PLabs to deliver train-the-trainer sessions for customers who want to self-enable their teams internally.
Handoff Readiness Ensure customers meet defined criteria before transitioning to CSM ownership, reducing early-stage churn risk and setting CSMs up for success.
